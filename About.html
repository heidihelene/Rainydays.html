About us

Rainydays has been run by dedicated and passionate outdoor enthusiasts for over 100 years.

It all started with the bicycle manufacturer Ole F. Bergan, an avid hunter and outdoorsman. He introduced and patented the backpack as we know it today.An invention among others Roald Amundsen recognized and made use of on his expeditions.

No other supplier of hiking equipment and technical clothing can match our outdoor and polar history, or our efforts to share the experiences with today's outdoor people. Since its inception in 1908, Bergans has for over a century played a crucial role in Norwegian outdoor life.
